Aasmi Recipe

Equipment

  • Pan
  • Aasmi strainer

Ingredients

Step 1: Ladies Fingers/Okra extract

  • 300 g Ladies fingers/Okra Fresh or frozen
  • 50 g Coconut (Fresh or dehydrated coconut) NOT COCONUT POWDER
  • 800 g Water

Step 2: Batter

  • 200 g Ready-made rice flour
  • 160 g Ladies fingers/Okra extract (From Step 1)
  • 180 g Coconut milk
  • 1/4 tsp Salt

Step 3: Syrup

  • 50 g White sugar
  • 20 g Water (Tap)
  • Drops food Colouring of your choice (Do not use GEL coloring)
  • Oil (Canola, coconut, sunflower oil) for frying

Instructions

Step 1: Ladies Fingers/Okra extract

  1. Place the coconut, water, okra in a blender and pulse it 3-4 times until blended. If the whole quantity doesn't fit in the blender at once, blend in batches.
  2. Strain the mixture first through a normal kitchen towel, then pass it through a fine kitchen towel. Leave the extract aside.
  3. Do not prepare this mixture too much in advance as the color might change, affecting the Aasmi color.

Step 2: Batter

  1. Sieve rice flour in a large bowl, add salt and coconut milk, mix well to form a dough.
  2. Add ladies fingers extract gradually to make a soft and silky batter. Adjust the consistency. Heat oil in a pot.
  3. Pour the batter using an Aasmi spoon in circular and zig-zag motions in hot oil. Fold it into a semi-circular shape, fry until crispy, and drain on a paper towel.
  4. Leave the Aasmi to cool in a dry place covered with a cloth for at least 3 days.

Step 3: Syrup

  1. Heat water, sugar, and food coloring in a saucepan until it forms a syrup (2-3 minutes). Avoid making it too stretchy or too thin.
  2. Cool the syrup for 2-3 minutes, then decorate the Aasmi using a spoon to draw designs.
  3. Enjoy the Aasmi on the day it's made.
