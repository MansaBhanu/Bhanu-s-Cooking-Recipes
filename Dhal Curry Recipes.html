Dhal Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 100 g Washed dhal
  • 10 g Sliced Onions
  • 1 Leaf Curry Leaves
  • 3 Capsules finely chopped Green chilies
  • 3 Leafs Pandan Leaves
  • 2 tbsp coconut Milk Powder dissolved in 150 ml of water
  • 0.25 tbsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp Chili powder
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 150 mL Water

Instructions

  1. Place a saucepan on the stove and transfer the 100g of dhal into the pan.
  2. Pour one cup of water (150ml) into the saucepan.
  3. Add 1 tsp of chili powder, salt, and 1/4 tsp of turmeric powder into the saucepan.
  4. Add 2-3 pieces of rampe/padan leaves and 8-12 curry leaves.
  5. Add the 3 finely chopped green chilies.
  6. Add the 10g of sliced onion.
  7. Pour and stir in the prepared Maggi coconut Milk Powder Mixture.
  8. Increase the heat of the stove and allow to boil.
  9. Serve into a curry dish and enjoy!
