Watalappan Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 250g palm jaggery (cane sugar) - this can be replaced with any other type available
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 70g brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cardamom powder
  • 50g chopped cashew nuts
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven at 120Â°C.
  2. Pour water into a pot and add jaggery on low heat.
  3. Once the jaggery has completely melted, remove from the heat and allow it to cool.
  4. Beat the eggs and gradually add the melted jaggery and coconut to it.
  5. Add and mix the cardamom powder, beans from the vanilla pod, and brown sugar.
  6. Pour the mixture into a baking tray and place this baking tray in another tray filled with water.
  7. Place the baking tray in the oven and bake for 50 minutes.
  8. It's done when itâ€™s wobbly in the center.
  9. Let it cool. It can also be refrigerated.
  10. Sprinkle with chopped cashew nuts and serve with vanilla ice-cream. This dessert can be served hot or cold.
