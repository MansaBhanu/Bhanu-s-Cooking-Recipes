Kottu Roti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 6 large jalapenos (or 3 anaheim peppers), halved and sliced (optional: remove seeds to make less spicy)
  • 1 cup leeks, sliced and washed (white part only)
  • 1/4 of a cabbage
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 cups shredded chicken (or rotisserie chicken)
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 lb. of chopped godhamba roti (or flour tortillas)
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1/3 cup of chicken stock
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. In a large wok or non-stick pan, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. When oil is heated, add onion and sautÃ© until softened.
  3. Add ginger and garlic and sautÃ© for a few seconds.
  4. Add all vegetables except carrots and a pinch of salt. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  5. Add chicken, cayenne pepper, curry powder, chicken stock, black pepper, and ground cinnamon.
  6. Stir-fry until vegetables soften.
  7. Add carrots and mix.
  8. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and season with a pinch of salt.
  9. Create a well in the middle of the vegetables and add eggs.
  10. Scramble eggs while they cook. When nearly cooked, mix with vegetables and chicken.
  11. Add shredded roti and mix until completely coated with spices and curry sauce.
  12. Cook for about 5 minutes and serve while hot.
